## Review expectations

- **Agent or workflow rule changes** — At least one maintainer approval before merge.
- **Docs only** — One maintainer or delegated reviewer.
- **Breaking handoffs or lifecycle order** — Discuss in an issue first; call out in `CHANGELOG.md` and announce to consuming teams.
